San Francisco Zoo

Volunteer

Allows people from all ages to help around the zoo from teaching children to gardening. Everyone must attend a mandatory orientation and training. Programs vary depending on the age groups. Must be 18 years old or older for adult volunteers and attend a volunteer orientation and pass a background check.

Volunteering at the San Francisco Zoo can be a fun, educational and rewarding experience. Volunteers support our mission by connecting guests to wildlife through informative talks, biofact presentations, and school tours. There are many volunteer opportunities in the zoo depending on your interest and age.

Information

The Zoo supports conserving the future nature and animals. They have popular summer and year round volunteer events that are designed to be fun and educating while developing life styles


For more info: http://www.sfzoo.org/learn/volunteer.htm
