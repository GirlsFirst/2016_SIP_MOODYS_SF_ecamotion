San Francisco Vikings

SOCCER

The low player-to-coach ratio enables the coaches to provide continuous attention and individual skills training. The camp also provides a variety of group exercises, small-sided games, and full field matches. In addition to this first-class training, every player will receive an official camp shirt, soccer ball, and bag. At the end of the week every player will also get a personal evaluation from the coaching staff

Information

Our coaches ensure that players of every age and ability level will have fun learning the game of soccer. All profits from the San Francisco Vikings Summer Camp support our other program activities and scholarship fund.
